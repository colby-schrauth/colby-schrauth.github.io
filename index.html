NOTES


My name is Colby Schrauth and I live in Half Moon Bay, California with my wife Carly and two Golden Retrievers: Timber and Kygo. I grew up in Eden, New York and attended university in Boca Raton, FL. I work on the Data Analytics & Science team at Square, and am Co-Founder of The San Francisco Data School.

Previously I worked at Weebly (we got acquired by Square), and led Data Analytics classes for General Assembly (San Francisco) and at Columbia University in New York for their MS in Applied Analytics program.

I use this site to organize thoughts, share my story, and hopefully help others.
