My name is Colby Schrauth and I live in Half Moon Bay, California with my wife Carly and two Golden Retrievers: Timber and Kygo. I grew up in Eden, New York and attended Florida Atlantic University in Boca Raton, FL. I currently work on the Product Operations team at Google, and am Co-Founder of San Francisco Data School.

Previously I worked at Weebly (we got acquired by Square), and led Data Analytics classes for General Assembly (San Francisco) and at Columbia University in New York for their MS in Applied Analytics program.

I use this site to organize thoughts, share my story, and hopefully help others. Thank you for visiting!
